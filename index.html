It takes a village

About

The King Educational Philosophy stems from an idealistic viewpoint. In order for society to value education, education has to be at the center of a society. This educational philosophy focuses on how to incorporate real world experience into the lives of students. One thing I have noticed about my students is their need to feel they have a place in this world. This combination increases student engagement as well as gives them a feeling of purpose.

To sum up my educational philosophy in a single phrase: “it takes a village” to educate a child. This goes with the concept that the community is responsible in helping a child grow. Students will have individual choice but work together with the community to foster the students’ talents. Students will work together with their teachers, parents, school systems, local and corporate businesses in order to achieve their goals.
